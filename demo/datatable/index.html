Here are all the metrics and properties that come with vRealize Operations Management Pack for Horizon (MP4H 2.0).

How to use this dynamic table:

  1. To hide column, do this.
  2. To filter based on specific value of a column do this.
  3. To filter based on any column, use the search box.
  4. Note: no 2 and 3 work together as join filter.
  5. To sort by any column, click on it.
  6. To sort multiple column, press shift then click any column.
